ABOUT ME

I have always had a deep love of computers and I still remember the first computer my parents got. An old blue Toshiba PC with fifa 97 where I used all my time either playing or trying to understand how it worked.

But it wasen't until 2014 that I found out that programming was actual a career possibility for me and I immediately took a career change away from marketing and towards development.

It was a long hard run and even though I love the people in programming, I never really fell in love with the teaching. I tried changing location to see if things would change but it was the same.

Finally I decided to go my own way. I dropped out, taking more focus to learning materials online and especially freecodecamp.com where you can learn to code for free through intuitiv challenges.

I am now already through the first challenges with this website and I have learned more web development through this course, than I ever did in school. I hope to see you all on the other side - happy coding!
